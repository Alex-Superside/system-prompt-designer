## citation_metadata_rules

Specifies the semantics and constraints of citation metadata attributes such as `data-id`, `data-type`, `data-title`, and `data-reasoning`: how they map to Supporting Data `contextItem` IDs, allowed types (`brand_brain` vs `initial_prompt`), reasoning length and content requirements, and restrictions on punctuation and formatting inside attribute values.
